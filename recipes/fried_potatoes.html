Fried Potatoes

Description:

Level up your side dish and make it the star of the show. Potatoes don't have to be a boring plate filler. With this recipe, you can create a dish that will cause your dinner guests to wonder "How did you make these?" The ultimate compliment to a home chef. All it takes is some chopping, boiling, and pan frying. Let the seasoning do the hard work for you.

The Recipe:

This is one of those dishes that you can start and finish early, place into a warming dish and keep warm in the oven while you're cooking everything else. Lets get started!

Ingredients:

  • 1 large cast iron skillet
  • 6 cups water
  • 1 lb of small golden potatoes (red works too)
  • 1/2 stick (4 oz) of unsalted butter
  • (Optional) 1/4 cup of beef tallow or bacon fat
  • 2 tablespoons kosher sea salt
  • 1 tablespoon fresh cracked pepper
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon paprika

Steps:

  1. Bring water to a boil (add a pinch of salt as boiling begins).
  2. Chop the potatoes into quarters.
  3. Boil potatoes for about 12-15 minutes. We want them softened but not breaking apart.
  4. Mix the salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and paprika together.
  5. Add butter (AND your beef tallow or bacon fat if available) to the cast iron skillet and turn burner on to medium.
  6. Drain water from potatoes thoroughly.
  7. Once the butter is melted completely, add potatoes to the skillet.
  8. Mix the potatoes around in the skillet to ensure that they are completely coated with fat on all sides.
  9. Sprinkle half of the seasoning over the top of the potatoes and let them simmer for about 5 minutes.
  10. Mix the potatoes around again, making sure all of the sides get a chance to touch the skillet and be seasoned.
  11. Sprinkle the rest of the seasoning over the top of the potatoes and let them simmer for 5 more minutes.
  12. Cook until you have the desired crispiness and texture, turn off the burner and move all of the contents of the skillet over to a dish for serving.
  13. Enjoy!
