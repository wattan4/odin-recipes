Buffalo Chicken Nachos

Description:

Add some flare to your next snack to accompany a couple beers and a football game. This dish satisfies our desires for spicy chicken wings, hearty nachos, and a salty crunch that keeps us around the table all afternoon long.

The Recipe:

It's quick and easy, so let's get right to it!

Ingredients:

  • Large cast iron skillet
  • 1 lb fresh chicken thighs
  • Your favorite chicken seasoning (recommend Bearded Butcher Original Seasoning
  • Your favorite tortilla chips
  • Sweet Baby Rays Creamy Buffalo Dip
  • 2 celery stalks chopped
  • 1/2 lb shredded mozzarella cheese
  • (Optional) 1/2 cup fresh crema

Steps:

  1. Heat grill to 500 degrees (charcoal or kamado style gives you the best flavor).
  2. Season the chicken thighs.
  3. Grill chicken thighs for about 5 minutes on each side, pull them off the grill once they reach an internal temperature of 165 degrees (F).
  4. Cool your grill down to 400 degrees (F).
  5. Chop chicken to desired size and consistency.
  6. Create a bed of tortilla chips covering the entire cast iron skillet.
  7. Pour a light layer of buffalo sauce onto the chips.
  8. Spread chicken and celery evenly across the chips.
  9. Cover all of the ingredients in the skillet with a liberal layer of shredded cheese.
  10. Pour another light layer of buffalo sauce (and crema if you'd like) on top of the cheese.
  11. Put skillet onto the grill and let it cook until the cheese is completely melted.
  12. Enjoy!
